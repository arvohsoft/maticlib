    Maticlib is an open-source project maintained by Arvoh Software and its community.

    Anubroto Ghose

    Main Maintainer & Lead Contributor

    Anubroto is the driving force behind Maticlib and current head of Arvoh Software. He focuses on the core architecture, graph engine performance, and the unified LLM interface.
